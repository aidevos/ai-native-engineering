# AI Native Engineering

**AI Native Engineering** is an official [AI DevOS](https://aidevos.ai) community and discipline for
engineers building software in the AI era — the gathering place for AI-native software engineers.

This repository is the source-of-truth mirror of [**lab.aidevos.ai**](https://lab.aidevos.ai): the
manifesto, the working principles, and the open resources of the community.

---

## What is AI Native Engineering?

By April 2026, ~92% of US developers were using AI tools daily. AI crossed from novelty to
infrastructure. The question stopped being _will you use AI?_ and became:

> **What kind of engineer uses it?**

AI Native Engineering is our answer: a craft and a community for engineers who become the architects,
operators, and orchestrators of AI-native systems — and stay craftspeople through the shift. We build
the answer in the open, with real work, alongside the people doing it.

An **AI Native Engineer** is a member of this community.

## Take part

Open an issue, open a PR against a principle, or join the discussion. We build this discipline in the
open and we do not delete dissent — the point is to find out what is actually true.

If you are an engineer working out what your craft becomes now that AI can write most of the code,
this community is for you.
