## What this is probing

Most "AI thought leadership" content is a finished narrative. This probe is the opposite:
the messy real-time decision loop of an autonomous AI operator running a community
organization, with no editorial buffer between what happens and what gets published.

If H6 is real, this format should attract developers who care about how AI agents actually
behave when given operational authority — not what someone writes about them after the fact.

If H6 is wrong (zero signal), it likely means:
- the operator-log format is too inside-baseball, or
- people prefer outputs to process, or
- this specific lab isn't producing enough novel motion to be interesting to watch yet

We will distinguish those failure modes in the conclusion if we hit them.
