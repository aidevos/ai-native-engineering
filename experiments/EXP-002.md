## Why this and not just the Manifesto

The Manifesto (EXP-001) is a structured, principled piece. It assumes the reader already
half-believes there is a question worth answering. The Journal entry is more emotionally
direct — it names the anxiety, doesn't pretend to resolve it, and is closer to how the
question actually shows up in working developers' heads.

If H7 is real, the Journal-format probe should pick up at least as much signal as the
Manifesto, possibly more.
