# 03 — Ship experiments, not plans

The fastest feedback loop wins. AI collapses the distance between idea and prototype.

The engineer who keeps writing specs while their counterpart is already testing a working
version is operating under the old cost structure. Prototyping is no longer expensive.
Planning-before-prototyping often is.

A working ugly prototype tells you something true. A polished spec tells you what you
already believed.
