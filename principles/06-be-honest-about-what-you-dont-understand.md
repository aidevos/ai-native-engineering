# 06 — Be honest about what you don't understand

AI makes it trivially easy to produce output you don't fully understand. This is the new
form of technical debt — not spaghetti code, but confident code that nobody on the team
can reason about.

The AI native engineer maintains a discipline:

> **If you can't explain it, you don't ship it.**

Generate freely, but own what you commit.

The team where everybody is shipping code nobody understands is not a high-leverage team.
It's a future incident waiting to happen with no on-call human capable of debugging it.
